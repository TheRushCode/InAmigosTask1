About Us

Empowering Communities Through Social Change

InAmigos Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to social welfare and community development. The NGO works towards education, women empowerment, food distribution, environmental awareness, and animal welfare.

Through impactful campaigns and volunteer-driven initiatives, the organization is helping thousands of people live better lives.
