Who We Are

A Movement Rooted
in Compassion

InAmigos Foundation was founded on September 23, 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla (Founder & CEO), headquartered in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh. It is a Section 8 registered non-profit, licensed by the Central Government of India.


Our foundation believes in the power of collective action — every contribution, every volunteer hour, every awareness campaign moves us closer to a more inclusive, compassionate, and empowered India.

✅ Section 8 Registered ✅ 80G & 12A Certified ✅ CSR-1 Registered ✅ NITI Aayog Listed ✅ ISO 9001:2015 Certified
Mr. Govind Shukla
Founder & CEO · InAmigos Foundation

"At InAmigos Foundation, we believe that every individual deserves dignity, opportunity, and compassion — and together, we have the power to make that a reality across India."

Our Ongoing Work

6 Projects, One Mission

Each project addresses a critical social need — from the classroom to the streets, from empowering women to protecting nature.

📚
Education
Project Bachpanshala

Ensuring quality, accessible education for underprivileged children across India. We believe every child deserves the chance to learn, grow, and dream — regardless of their economic background.

🌟 Nurturing Young Minds, Building Bright Futures
🍚
Food & Clothing
Project Sewa

Serving humanity by distributing nutritious food and warm clothing to the underprivileged. No one should go to bed hungry or face the cold without protection.

💚 Serving Humanity with Compassion
👩‍💼
Women Empowerment
Project Udaan

Empowering women through skill development workshops, financial literacy programs, and entrepreneurship support — creating pathways to independence and confidence.

🦋 Soaring Towards a Brighter Future
🐾
Animal Welfare
Project Jeev

Dedicated to animal rescue, protection, and feeding — because compassion knows no species. We work to alleviate the suffering of stray and injured animals in our communities.

💜 Empowering Lives, Spreading Compassion
🌱
Environment
Project Prakriti

Driving environmental conservation through tree-planting drives, awareness campaigns, and sustainability initiatives. We are committed to protecting the planet for future generations.

🌿 Plant for a Better Tomorrow
🛠️
Skill Development
Project Vikas

Enhancing employability by providing vocational training and skill development programs — equipping individuals with practical tools to secure meaningful livelihoods and achieve economic independence.

🔴 Developing Skills, Transforming Lives
